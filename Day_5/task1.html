India
  • India is a vast and diverse country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country in the world by area. India is the most populous democracy in the world. New Delhi is the capital of India. Mumbai is the financial capital of the country. India has a rich history that dates back thousands of years.

  • The Indus Valley Civilization was one of the world’s oldest civilizations. India is known for its unity in diversity. Many religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism originated here. People of different cultures, languages, and traditions live together. Hindi and English are the official languages of the central government.

  • India has 28 states and 8 union territories. The national flag has three colors: saffron, white, and green. The Ashoka Chakra is in the center of the flag. The national anthem is “Jana Gana Mana.” The national animal is the tiger. The national bird is the peacock. The national flower is the lotus. India is famous for its festivals like Diwali, Holi, Eid, and Christmas.

  • Agriculture is an important occupation in India. India is also known for its achievements in science and technology. The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has made the country proud. The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Indian cuisine is popular all over the world. Yoga and Ayurveda originated in India. India believes in peace and non-violence.
